ABOUT US

Throttle Manila

Throttle Manila is your premier destination for luxury and high-performance supercars in the Philippines. Founded with a passion for speed, elegance, and unmatched automotive engineering, Throttle Manila is dedicated to bringing the finest selection of supercars to enthusiasts and collectors.

Our Mission

At Throttle Manila, our mission is to provide exclusive access to the world’s most prestigious supercars. We aim to be the leading authority in the Philippine luxury automotive market, offering exceptional quality, unparalleled expertise, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

What We Offer

  • Exclusive Supercars: We curate a select inventory of the most sought-after supercars, featuring brands known for their performance, design, and prestige.
  • Personalized Service: Our knowledgeable team offers personalized consultations to help you find the perfect supercar that matches your lifestyle and preferences.
  • Luxury Experience: From the moment you step into our showroom to the delivery of your vehicle, we ensure a seamless and luxurious experience.
  • Community and Events: Engage with a community of like-minded supercar enthusiasts through exclusive events, drives, and meetups.
